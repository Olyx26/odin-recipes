Brownies

Description

These rich and fudgy classic brownies are the ultimate chocolate lover’s dessert. They’re simple to prepare and come out with a shiny top and soft, gooey center.

Perfect for any occasion, these brownies can be enjoyed on their own or with a scoop of ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (40g) cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt the butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs.
  6. Let cool before slicing into squares.
