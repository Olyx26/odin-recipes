Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are chewy, buttery, and filled with rich chocolate chips. They're super easy to make and perfect for a quick treat.

Whether you're a baking beginner or just craving something sweet, these cookies never disappoint.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (170g) chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter with both sugars.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla, and stir until combined.
  4. Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden.
  8. Let cool before serving.
