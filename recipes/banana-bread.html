Banana Bread

Description

This moist and flavorful banana bread is a great way to use up overripe bananas. It has a soft texture and a naturally sweet taste, making it a comforting treat.

Enjoy it for breakfast, as a snack, or even as a light dessert with a cup of tea or coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup (75g) melted butter
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter.
  3. Add sugar, egg, and vanilla. Stir until combined.
  4. Mix in baking soda and salt.
  5. Add flour and stir just until the batter is mixed.
  6. Pour into the loaf pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool before slicing and serving.
